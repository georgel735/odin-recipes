George's Recipe

Description

A dish as classically Asian as Kung Fu and General Tso's Chicken. Enjoy the powerful taste of George Lin's famous Fried Rice.

Ingredients

  • Oil from the Mississipi River
  • Rice from a struggling sweatshop employee's dinner
  • An assortment of veggies stolen from the supermarket
  • Meat unknowingly borrowed from a neighbors farm

Steps

  1. Tell Mike there's NO safe word tonigth and oil your frying pan up like there's no tomorrow
  2. Throw in yo meat first.
  3. Then, after you make sure your meat is cooked, throw in some veggies.
  4. Lastly put in some rice and stir everything for 5-10 minute.
  5. TADA!!! Nice, yummy fried rice.
